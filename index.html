Problem

India generates over 3.5 million tonnes of plastic waste annually, of which only 60% is recycled. The rest pollutes land and water bodies. Companies and municipalities struggle to find efficient recycling solutions.

Solution

Clearit is a plastic waste brokerage platform that connects waste producers with certified recyclers. Using AI for sorting and blockchain for traceability, we help meet ESG goals and promote a circular economy.

  • AI-powered waste classification
  • Blockchain for traceable transactions
  • Real-time ESG tracking
  • Recycling and upcycling into usable materials
