Problem

India generates 3.5M+ tonnes of plastic waste yearly; only 60% is recycled. Unmanaged plastic harms land, water, businesses, and community health.

We connect waste producers with recyclers to support sustainability and cut pollution.

Solution

Plastic Waste Brokerage Platform - Connects businesses and cities with recyclers. Uses React, Node, AWS, AI sorting, and blockchain tracking.

  • Certified recycler matching
  • ESG compliance dashboard
  • Plastic shredding & upcycling

Target Customers

Primary: Businesses & municipalities with plastic waste.

Secondary: Consumers seeking eco-friendly products.

Key Features & Benefits

  • Real-time waste tracking
  • AI-based sorting & classification
  • Blockchain for transparency
  • Upcycled raw material marketplace

Market Size

India’s plastic recycling market TAM is ~$15B, SAM ~$6–8B. SOM: ₹25–40 crore in 2 years.

Revenue Model

  • Transaction fees per ton of processed waste
  • Bulk processing charges for large recyclers
  • Consulting & ESG compliance services
